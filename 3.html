One particular book which is recommended reading is The Street Lawyer by John Grisham. This book is about a lawyer who begins re-evaluating his priorities in life when a bad incident occurs whithin his law firm. Consequently, he becomes acquinted with the inner city streets, and realizes the harsh existence of the homeless, and vows to give them a chance in the courts.The Street Lawyer is a great book. It is well written and interesting. Other books by John Grisham include the Firm.The Pelican Brief and The Client
